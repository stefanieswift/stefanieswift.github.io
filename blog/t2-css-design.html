Padding, Margins, and Borders, Oh My

A beginner's guide

January 25

Padding

Adding padding to your code creates space between your content and the border of the content divider.

Margin

Margin is the space between the content divider and the border of the page.

Border

A border is the outside of your content. You can set your border to have certain properties or the border will just be the outside of your shape.
